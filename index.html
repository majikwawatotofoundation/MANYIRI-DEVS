We Are Manyiri Developers

Manyiri isn't just a team — it's a movement. We're a growing network of young creatives from Kenya’s coast and beyond. You’ll find us in Mombasa, Kilifi, Ukunda, Watamu — even where there’s no office or map pin. We show up, we create, and we grow together.

Web Developers who bring ideas to life with clean, powerful code.

Digital Campaigners & Social Media Managers who build real online presence.

Photographers who capture culture, identity, and moments.

Content Creators who influence with originality.

And yes — we’ve got mentor support in the background guiding it all.
